Whose The Food Lover

Well I tried to explain my relationship with food but I think I have not done good enough :). Food for me is like a stress buster, a shoulder to cry on while I am sad, a true friend indeed. When I am stressed I turn to food , when I am happy I turn to food, when I am confused I for sure turn to food. I don't eat because I want to or have to eat. I eat because I love food. It will be completely wrong if I say I only love to eat , I love to cook too. And If I go with people what they say about my hand cooked food in two words "It's awesome !!!!". I love experimenting with flavors , spices . I love visiting places and try different cuisines , try to understand the food they eat.
